# QUESTIONNAIRE DESIGN

---
Question: S1
Type: Single
Label / Text: Please indicate your gender
Options:

  1. Male
  2. Female
Logic: All respondents

---
Question: S2
Type: Single
Label / Text: Please indicate your age group
Options:

  1. Under 18 -> Terminate respondent
  2. 18 – 24
  3. 25 – 34
  4. 35 – 44
  5. 45 or above
Logic: All respondents

---
Question: Q1
Type: Single
Label / Text: Do you currently nurture a pet?
Options:

  1. Yes
  2. No
Logic: Ask for all respondents (who are not terminated)

---
Question: Q2
Type: Multi
Label / Text: Which type(s) of pet do you nurture? (Multiple answers allowed)
Options:

  1. Cat
  2. Dog
  3. Fish
  4. Bird
  5. Other -> Specify: ___
Logic: Ask if Q1 == 1 (Yes)

---
Question: Q3
Type: Single
Label / Text: How long have you been nurturing a pet?
Options:

  1. Less than 1 year
  2. 1 – 3 years
  3. 4 – 6 years
  4. More than 6 years
Logic: Ask if Q1 == 1 (Yes)

---
Question: Q4
Type: Single
Label / Text: Which brand of pet food do you purchase most often?
Options:

  1. Brand A
  2. Brand B
  3. Brand C
  4. Other brand
Logic: Ask if Q1 == 1 (Yes)

---
Question: Q5
Type: Number
Label / Text: Approximately how much (in USD) do you spend on pet food per month?
Logic: Ask if Q1 == 1 (Yes)

---
Question: Q6
Type: Single
Label / Text: Overall, how satisfied are you with the pet food brand you use most often?
Options:

  1. Very satisfied
  2. Satisfied
  3. Neutral
  4. Dissatisfied
  5. Very dissatisfied
Logic: Ask if Q1 == 1 (Yes)

---
Question: Q7
Type: Single
Label / Text: Are you interested in switching to a different pet food brand in the next 6 months?
Options:

  1. Yes, definitely
  2. Yes, possibly
  3. No
Logic: Ask if Q1 == 1 (Yes)

---
Question: D1
Type: Single
Label / Text: What is your highest level of education?
Options:

  1. High school or below
  2. Vocational / Diploma
  3. Bachelor's degree
  4. Postgraduate degree
Logic: Ask for all respondents (who are not terminated)
